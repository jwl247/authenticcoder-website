The Story Behind the Code

It started with Laurie.

My sweetheart is high-functioning autistic. I built Life First App specifically for her — a relentless accountability companion, 10 AI-powered modules, the best security I knew how to build.

When vendor lock-in threatened everything I made for her, I didn't have a choice. I built the foundation myself. That's how Phoenix DevOps OS was born.

I'm a blue-collar ironworker. A house full of children made the man I am. Laurie makes me want to be better than that man. These projects are labors of love — both of them.

"A man learns early to be invaluable when you have a house full of children. They made me the man I am — and make me want to be a better man than I am, for Laurie."
— jwl247, ironworker & open source developer

Phoenix DevOps OS

A full DevOps operating system built from the ground up — one pipeline, every platform, everything tracked. Every file, package, config, and dependency that enters the system is intercepted, registered, and cataloged with a permanent hex identity. Built by one ironworker because the alternatives weren't good enough for the people he loves.
